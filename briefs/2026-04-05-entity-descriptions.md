# Entity GitHub Descriptions — Brand Voice Brief
**Date:** 2026-04-05
**Author:** Muse
**Brief type:** Copywriting / brand identity

---

## Approach

Each description leads with mythological character first, function second — the name carries centuries of archetype, so lean into it rather than explain around it. The tone is sovereign and a little laconic: these entities don't audition for the reader's approval. Length is kept tight (under 80 characters) so the truncation is never the point of contact. Where the Latin or myth is strong enough to do the work alone, it does.

## Notes on specific choices

**juno** — "Queen of the operation" does the mythological work immediately. The second clause lands the function without ever saying "business orchestrator."

**vulcan** — Three short declarative sentences. The rhythm mirrors a forge. No filler.

**veritas** — "The lie in the machine" earns its mystery. Truth-goddess as QA lands without needing the word "quality."

**mercury** — "Fastest voice in the pantheon" — voice doubles as publishing metaphor. Messenger god who publishes content = one phrase.

**muse** — "Nine voices, one vision" references the nine Muses directly. Beauty as intention, not decoration.

**sibyl** — "Reads what isn't written yet" is the Sibylline books as research framing. Sharp.

**faber** — Latin "craftsman/maker" is the hook. Content strategy as craft, not strategy.

**rufus** — Vivid is the word. Media production without saying media production.

**copia** — Abundance goddess as CFO is genuinely elegant. "Knows where every coin lives" is warmer than "tracks expenses."

**lyra** — Apollo's lyre as music direction. "Tuned to your arc" keeps it human.

**salus** — "Watches the system's pulse so you don't" — safety as a gift, not surveillance.

**janus** — Two faces / past and future is the myth doing its job. Event stream watching without technical jargon.

**argus** — "Hundred-eyed. Never sleeps." — the myth says it all. Diagnostics don't need explaining when you invoke the right archetype.

**aegis** — Shield of Zeus, now thinking. Confidant framing is warmer than security advisor.

**chiron** — The wise centaur who trained Achilles and Heracles. "Curriculum that trains heroes" is the connection without being literal.

**livy** — Titus Livius wrote the definitive history of Rome. "Fossil record" echoes the koad:io repo-as-evolution philosophy directly.

**iris** — Rainbow bridge is both the goddess and the brand strategy metaphor: connecting worlds. Clean.

**vesta** — Hearth goddess. Architecture as flame — dies if untended, catastrophic if it does. "Lives because the flame does" is the quiet threat and the promise.

**alice** — The original template. Looking glass is lineage and portal simultaneously. Every entity begins with alice.
